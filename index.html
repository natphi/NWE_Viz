Direct NorthWestern Energy exports work without modification when they include hourly `DATE`, `START TIME`, and `USAGE` columns.

Required schema
Use a NorthWestern Energy detailed usage export with hourly rows containing `DATE`, `START TIME`, and `USAGE`. The standard NorthWestern CSV export works as-is.
How to access detailed usage in My Energy Account
  1. Log in to My Energy Account.
  2. Select `Billing` from the menu.
  3. Click `View Detailed Usage`.
  4. Click the 'Download my data' button in the top left.
  5. Select the time period you want to review.
  6. Download your data using the blue button at the bottom right.

This page runs entirely in your browser. Your CSV is processed locally and is never uploaded anywhere.
